Whole Life Guaranteed Quote

Problem

The current quote is outdated, and in need of a refresh. While the current style is working, make it fit the new design system and make necessary improvements

Discovery

Site Analysis

  • Using the existing quote as a starting point, I looked at how it was performing using Google Analytics and other analytics provided by a 3rd party partner
  • Most people were getting a single quote
  • The most popular amount quoted was 10,000

Inital Usability Study

  • I also performed a usability study on the current quote:
  • I found that people could not find the option to perform a dual quote, so they were doing a single quote twice, even after selecting they wanted a dual quote
  • Users thought the design for the current quote was “too clunky”
  • Users want the ability to quickly perform a “re-quote” so that they can check to see what meets their budget
  • With $25,000 set as a default, testers thought that this amount was the minimum face amount they could purchase, until they performed their first quote and saw that they could go lower in the results
  • Testers wanted an option to input how much they could afford monthly, and the quote return how much coverage they would receive

Quote Wireframe Summary

  • The designs underwent a round of usability studies with 5 participants. With the tenth input design testing highest and the third output design emerging as the one that was best for the user.
  • My goal for the whiteboard exercises were to reduce the user’s cognitive load. The “fill in the blank” quote required the user to engage in a read and answer style until the quote entry was complete.
  • In the previous design, the user did not know what to expect next when pressing the “submit” button. The design would automatically scroll down the page, to reflect the quote results.
  • I discovered that if I included only the most pertinent entry fields and displayed in a vertical list format, it allowed the user to quickly and accurately complete the quote.

Prototype

  • The compact design of quote allowed it to be developed on a modular platform, enabling it to be placed in multiple places, such as the main website, and any marketing landing pages using the same codebase, cutting down the time it takes to spin up a new webpage with the quote included on it.

Internal Validation

Internal Review

  • I held a final design review with the Product Owner and the major stakeholders in the business
  • The design was well received, and they liked that this solution was allowed the customer to input their information and see the results without feeling like they were being taken to a new page
  • The business group also liked the simplicity of this design, noting that the previous quoting design felt cumbersome comparatively

External Validation

Usability Study

  • I performed a final usability study, before moving in to the final development phase, with 5 users who would align with the persona.
  • This usability study shows that the testers were able to move through the quote faster than the first group, and they had an understanding on where the quote results would appear and what next steps to take.
  • In the final design, the proximity of the quote results in relation to the four entry fields gives the user context as to where to look after pressing the “get quote” button
